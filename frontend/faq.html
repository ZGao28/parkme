What is ParkMe?

ParkMe is a simple to use utility app for when you looking for a parking location in busy places. If you are in need of a place to park, or if you want to help the community by taking a photo of a parking sign (and you get POINTS!), then ParkMe is perfect for you.

What inspired ParkMe?

The idea of ParkMe was concieved when a young Anthony Aoun couldn't find any parking spots downtown, and didn't know what to do, nor where to go anymore.. Let's be honest - finding a parking spot has always been an issue, and we are all waiting for that solution to solve this big problem.

Our Goal

To make parking in busy locations more convienent and less confusing for everyone! Going out downtown with friends/family should not = more stress!
